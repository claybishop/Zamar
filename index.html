Field Equipment in Use

We are ever expanding our field equipment but currently we run a 2016 Ford & 2017 Dodge Altec AT37G Aerial Trucks with a 40’ reach. We splice out of a 2017 Dodge Promaster Sprinter vans or 2017 Fiber splicing trailers. The fiber optic splicers we user are either INNO or Fujikura all new with current calibration reports. We only test with EXFO Platforms, With Exfo Test platforms being required by our major customers like Comcast, Google & Zayo its easier to only use their products. EXFO maintains the highest quality customer test package so it’s an easy choice for us to only use the best on the market.
